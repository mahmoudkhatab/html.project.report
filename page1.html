Computer Engineering Role in COVID-19 Pandemic

Computer engineers can develop and create many educational programs on the Internet so that students can stay in their homes without needing to go to schools and universities to obtain education from their homes as well as create and develop some mobile applications and web pages that enable many people to work in their homes without the need to leave the house Likewise, they can develop and design interesting games and educational programs for children to keep them entertained, and we can also create educational platforms on the Internet and put courses on them in various fields to make people enjoy sitting at home
