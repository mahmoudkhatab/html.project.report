Applications of Emerging Technologies in the COVID-19 Pandemic.

S No Emerging technology Applications in the COVID-19 pandemic
1) Artificial intelligence (AI) Detecting virus, individuals with fever, and suspected symptoms of COVID-19 through the integration of thermal imaging, AI, and accordingly advice for the treatment and this has brought down the time of genetic detection to minutes.
2) Cloud computing All necessary information is stored in the computing platform and made available to enable a huge amount of computing power for users with the help of the Internet and help in making real-time decisions in disease modeling. The software can be used with blockchain and other tools to model vital facilities requirements on a different level, from hospital to people
3) Big data Provide storage capacity for extensive data of the population in a format that can be used efficiently for analysis and necessary action can be taken toward the prevention of disease transmission, movement, health monitoring, and prevention system
4) Telemedicine A patient can have a consultation from professionals on their medical conditions through video calls, avoiding the need for a hospital visiting and thus helping the social distancing and man-to-man contact and disease transmission. therefor, these remote consultations are now possible with using better telecom infrastructure with virtual reality
5) Smartphone apps Uses high-speed network and help to track strategic locations, infected patients and registering the data and modeling of disease outcomes as per the application software and other technologies can also be integrated with the software.
6) Robotics Undertakes repetitive jobs with precision and reliability in the hazardous environment of infectious disease in and around the hospitals and can make an intelligent decision with inputs from the population data analyzed through AI
7) Internet of things All devices are connected to the internet in the hospital and strategic locations. Thus, these connected devices help to inform the medical staff of any errors and change of requirements during the treatment process (similar to the factories of the future).
